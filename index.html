In Agnihotri group of institutes organize the tech age program in the Agnihotri campus Nagthana, Wardha. All the teaching staff are involved in the program and also the student. This program encourages the student and opens the path for new ideas. Developing the student skill and knowledge is the primary purpose of the program.

    1.Individual participation.

    3.Duration : 10 mins

  • Poster presentation

    1.Comple poster in A2 size.
    2.Should be based on given theme.
    3.Submission : on 12th April before 11:00 am at registration counter

  • Chess

    1.Only 16 entries.
    2.Touch to move.
    3.The match will be of 20 min only.

  • Carrom

    1.Individual participation.
    2.All type of moves are allowed.
    3.Striker should be placed on line.
    4.umpire decision will be final

    2.Submit your PPT before 10:00 am in seminar hall.
    3.Presentation time limit is 5-10 mins
